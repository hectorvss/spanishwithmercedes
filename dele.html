What is the DELE exam?

The DELE (Diploma de Español como Lengua Extranjera) is the official internationally recognised Spanish language qualification, awarded by the Instituto Cervantes on behalf of the Spanish Ministry of Education. It is accepted by universities, employers and governments worldwide as proof of Spanish proficiency.

The exam is available at six levels — from A1 (absolute beginner) to C2 (master level) — following the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR). Exams are held twice a year at official exam centres around the world.

Exam skills tested

📖
Reading (Comprensión de lectura)
Multiple reading tasks testing comprehension of authentic texts, articles and documents at your level.
🎧
Listening (Comprensión auditiva)
Audio recordings of conversations, news, interviews and announcements with comprehension questions.
✍️
Writing (Expresión escrita)
Written tasks including emails, essays, reports and formal letters appropriate to your level.
🗣️
Speaking (Expresión oral)
Face-to-face oral tasks with an examiner — describing images, role plays and presenting your opinion.

Mercedes's top 8 DELE preparation tips

1
Start with official sample papersDownload past papers from the Instituto Cervantes website. Familiarise yourself with the exact format, timing and question types before anything else.
2
Practice timed reading every dayDELE reading tasks are time-pressured. Train yourself to skim for gist and scan for detail — don't read every word.
3
Listen to authentic Spanish dailyPodcasts, news radio, TV series. The more variety of accents and speeds you hear, the more confident you'll be on exam day.
4
Write model answers and check themPractice writing the exact type of text required at your level. Use connectors, formal register and varied vocabulary.
5
Record yourself speakingThe oral exam is where many students lose marks. Record yourself doing the speaking tasks and listen back critically.
6
Don't ignore grammar — but don't obsessAccuracy matters, but so does fluency. Aim for communicative competence, not perfection.
7
Join a study groupPractice speaking with other DELE candidates. Mercedes's YouTube community is a great place to find partners.
8
Use Mercedes's free DELE materialsDownload the free mock exams and preparation PDFs from our Free PDF section. They are specifically designed for DELE success.
