Who is
Mercedes?

Mercedes is a native Spanish teacher from Spain with over 15 years of experience teaching Spanish to students from all over the world. She is the creator of the YouTube channel Spanish Lessons With Mercedes, where she shares structured, engaging lessons for every level — completely free.

Her method combines clear grammar explanations with real conversational practice and deep cultural context, so you don't just learn Spanish — you truly understand it.
