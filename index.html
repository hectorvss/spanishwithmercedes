Hola, soy

Mercedes

I am a native Spanish teacher from Spain, with more than 15 years of experience teaching Spanish to students of all nationalities and levels — from complete beginners to advanced learners preparing for the DELE exam.

Throughout my career I have taught in schools, academies and private classes, working with students from across the world. This experience has given me a deep understanding of how different native languages shape the way people learn — and what each student truly needs to make progress.

I trained in linguistics and the teaching of Spanish as a foreign language, and I have never stopped learning. I believe that teaching Spanish is not just about grammar — it is about opening a door to a new way of seeing the world.
